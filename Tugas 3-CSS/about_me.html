ditulis oleh feliciakireina. pada 1 Maret 2024

Hello and welcome! My name is Felicia Kireina Salim, and I'm delighted to share a little bit about myself with you.
Born in the vibrant city of Medan on February 9, 2005, my journey took an early turn when, at just two years old, my family moved to Palembang. This beautiful place has been my home ever since, shaping my experiences and outlook on life.
Now, at 19 years old, I find myself continuously exploring and embracing new challenges. One of my longest-held passions is drawing. It's been a part of me since my elementary school days. While I humbly consider myself not particularly exceptional at it, drawing has always provided me with a profound sense of joy and a creative outlet for expressing my thoughts and emotions.
